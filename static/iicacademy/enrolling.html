COURSE PRE-REQUISITES

The following skills, education and experience are required for program entry:

  • A high school diploma plus two years of additional education focused on GIS, geography, physics, mathematics, computer science or IT from a technical college, institute or university.
  • English proficiency.

If you are interested in attending this course but do not have the requirements listed above, please contact our friendly team at hydrographicsurveyor@iicacademy.com and we can discuss how these could be achieved.
