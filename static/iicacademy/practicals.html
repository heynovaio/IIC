Practicals

Practicals are, you guessed it, the hands on component of the program. You will travel to one of the locations listed to carry out the practical exercises, from boatmanship to data management, going through the set up of sensors, positioning and data capture. The goal is to put to use all the theory learned through the remote learning period.

Practicals are scheduled to take an intensive period of three weeks and the Comprehensive Final Field Project would start immediately after.
